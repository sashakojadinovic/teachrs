What is Chemistry??

An Introduction to Matter, Substances, and How the World Works

Hi Mark, is there any chemistry between us?

Hi Sara, Chemistry is everywhere. In us, and all around us.

Sara: That's not exactly what I meant, but go on—I like listening to you. :)

Mark: Well then, listen up. Chemistry is a fascinating science.

Sara: I know every science has something it studies. I’d love for my favorite subject—you—to explain the subject of this one. :)

Mark: Chemistry is the study of matter. That’s what it focuses on.

Sara: And what exactly is matter?

Mark: Matter is anything that takes up space and has mass. Honestly, it’s harder to think of something that isn’t matter than something that is.

Sara: But what about love, Mark? Love doesn’t have mass or take up space, even though it sometimes feels like butterflies are taking over my stomach.

Mark: You're right. Love is an emotion — it’s not matter. But everything that objects around us are made of, including ourselves, is matter.
